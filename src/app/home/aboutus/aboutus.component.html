About US

From the founder:

I admit to being a tree-hugger. I enjoy and feel connected to nature and accept a responsibility to take care of it. To that end I have always recycled and reused to the best of my ability. These efforts have led to teasing from my children because while on road trips I will drive recyclable materials across state lines to proper receptacles so I don't add them to a landfill, and support recycling efforts (although major change to the entire plastic situation is vital at this point!)

6 months ago I was in a fast food restaurant and noticed the bowl I was eating from had a recycle symbol on it, but the restaurant did not have a place to put recycling. There were two choices; put it in the trash or take it home to re-use and then add to my recycling. Of course the choice is clear for me, but I realize many people would just put it in the trash. I thought to myself, "someone should make an app that lets customers support restaurants who are using sustainable practices." Later I was at a coffee shop and saw that many people who were engaged in lengthy visits with friends had coffee in disposable cups and I thought; "coffee shops should do a better job of offering mugs for those who are staying on site and someone should make an app so customers know where those coffee shops are." Then I decided that someone would be me!

During this journey I have realized even more ways that I can be more conscious of my actions and do better, especially if I am going to be so engaged in supporting and encouraging the philosophy of sustainable practices - through WOLO.

Please join me as we work together to do what we can to make a difference in taking better care of the world we live in.
