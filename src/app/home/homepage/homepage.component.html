WOLO serves many purposes:

  • Provide information to encourage support of businesses who use sustainable practices
  • Crowd-source: users can refer earth-friendly businesses to be documented on WOLO, and also advise us of any discrepancies listed for a business
  • Allow businesses to be examples to each other
  • Donate to environmental causes

  • Provide information regarding news and events
  • Encourage the philosophy of reduce, reuse, recycle, repair, etc.
